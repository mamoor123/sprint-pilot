# SprintPilot — AI Sprint Command Center

> **Plan sprints in minutes, not meetings.**

A premium, AI-powered sprint planning tool that goes beyond basic Kanban boards. SprintPilot combines intelligent health scoring, risk detection, async team voting, and AI-generated standups into one beautiful command center.

## 🚀 Live Demo

**[Try SprintPilot Live](https://mamoor123.github.io/sprint-pilot/)**

## ✨ What Makes SprintPilot Different

### 🎯 Sprint Health Score (0-100)
Real-time health dashboard that calculates sprint risk based on weighted progress (done=100%, review=80%, in-progress=50%), blocked items, and velocity trends. Instantly see if your sprint is on track, at risk, or critical.

### 🛡️ Risk Radar
Automatically detects sprint risks:
- Critical stories not started
- Large stories (8+ pts) at risk of spillover
- Active stories with no assignee
- Sprint falling behind expected progress

### ⚡ Quick Wins Finder
AI identifies high-impact, low-effort stories (≤3 pts, high priority) that can be completed quickly for instant momentum.

### 🤖 AI Story Estimator
Paste any user story and get instant story point estimates with complexity analysis, confidence scores, and breakdown suggestions for large stories.

### 📊 AI Sprint Suggestion
Based on your backlog and team velocity, AI suggests which stories to pull into the sprint for optimal capacity utilization.

### 🗳️ Async Sprint Vote
Team members vote on backlog stories asynchronously. No more 2-hour planning meetings. AI aggregates votes and recommends the sprint lineup.

### 📋 AI Standup Generator
Generates daily standup summaries from story status changes — completed, in progress, in review, up next, and blockers.

### 👥 Team Workload Balancer
Visual workload cards showing each team member's capacity, assigned points, and story list. Instantly spot who's overloaded vs underutilized.

### 📈 Velocity Tracking
Bar charts showing sprint-over-sprint velocity trends and donut charts for story distribution by priority.

### ⌘ Command Palette (Ctrl+K)
Linear-style command palette for instant navigation, story search, and quick actions. Just press Ctrl+K and type.

### 🎨 Premium Design
